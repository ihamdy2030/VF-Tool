Activities & Required PPE

🧗 Working at Height
PPE: Harness, Lanyard, Helmet, Safety Shoes
EN: EN 361, EN 355, EN 397, EN ISO 20345
⚡ Electrical Work
PPE: Insulated Gloves, Dielectric Shoes, Face Shield
EN: EN 60903, EN 50321, EN 166
🏗️ Lifting Operations
PPE: Helmet, Gloves, Safety Shoes, Reflective Vest
EN: EN 397, EN 388, EN ISO 20345
🚧 Confined Space
PPE: Respirator, Harness, Gas Detector
EN: EN 140, EN 143, EN 361
🚗 Driving
PPE: Seat Belt
EN: Vehicle Safety Standards
🔥 Hot Work (Welding & Cutting)
PPE: Welding Helmet, Leather Gloves, Apron, Face Shield
EN: EN 175, EN 379, EN ISO 11611
⛏️ Excavation / Civil Work
PPE: Helmet, Safety Shoes, Reflective Vest
EN: EN 397, EN ISO 20345
📡 RF Work
PPE: RF Monitor, Helmet, Safety Shoes
EN: RF Exposure Standards
📦 Manual Handling
PPE: Gloves, Safety Shoes
EN: EN 388, EN ISO 20345
🧪 Chemical Work
PPE: Chemical Gloves, Respirator, Goggles
EN: EN 374, EN 140, EN 166
